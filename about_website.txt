Artists with unknown names are not listed, although they might be featured in band photographs. 

Instruments played by the artist are listed in order of relevance. For instance, an artist who for the most part plays guitar and less frequently drums, will have "guitar, drums" listed. 

Photo descriptions list, if available, L - R, time, date and scenario. 

URL lists the most representative web site. It can be band's or artist's own domain, Wikipedia, YouTube, etc. Facebook is listed separately. 

Prev and Next buttons on individual band and artist pages show all bands and artists, in alphabetical order. 

Dates are in the format DD-MM-YYYY. Unknown dates are displayed as XX-XX-XX. Known year with missing day and month is displayed as XX-XX-YYYY.
