Five archival songs by Tommy Thayer and Jamie St. James, recorded in late 1988/early 1989 and finally issued in 2022 as a self-released CD. The core lineup is Thayer (guitars) and St. James (vocals, drums), with guest parts by Jeff Labansky, Mitch Perry, and Patrick Young. The material was restored from surviving DAT and cassette sources after the original analog tapes had deteriorated beyond use.
